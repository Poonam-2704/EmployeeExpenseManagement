What is Expense Management?

Expense management refers to the process through which businesses handle and monitor the expenses incurred by employees during work-related activities. It involves tracking, approving, reimbursing, and reporting on business-related expenses, ensuring that the process is efficient, accurate, and in compliance with company policies.
